About Us

XPLORE, led by the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering at the Future Institute of Technology, aims to spread and improve technical skills in new Electronics advancements. It welcomes students from various fields and academic levels to get involved and take an active part.

Participants are encouraged to be creative and do things differently. Plus, having alumni and teachers as guides adds real-life experience. In the end, Xplore shows how working together and following your passion can shape the future of technology. It brings together a community of people who love exploring new ideas and making them happen.
