Rules

  1. Participants have to submit their Project overview with the Project Title and Functional Block Diagram within 500 words in PDF format (during online registration).
  2. Elimination will be done on the basis of a submitted project overview which should contain some innovative idea.
  3. Participants can register themselves in a team of TWO-FOUR members (A group should not exceed FOUR members). Each group should have a name.
  4. Only the registered group members are allowed on the day of the event and no change in group members will be allowed.
  5. At least one member should be always present during the project demonstration.
  6. One person cannot register in multiple teams or else those teams will be disqualified.
  7. The project presented on the day of the event should be the same as the one in the overview or else the team will be disqualified.
  8. The project must be presented along with a poster on the day of the event. The poster must contain:
    • Block diagram
    • Applications of the project
    • Estimated cost
  9. Each participant must carry their college ID card.
  10. Registration Fees – ₹200/- Per Team. Please mention team name in notes while paying online.
